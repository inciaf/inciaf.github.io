[Update: I have graduated, and now work at Google.]

Hi! I'm Suvinay Subramanian, currently a graduate student at MIT, working in the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL), advised by Daniel Sanchez.

I work in computer architecture and computer systems, with a focus on large-scale multicores and parallel programming models. My current research seeks to develop new programming models and architectural mechanisms, to exploit erstwhile unexplored forms of parallelism in applications.

Previously, I completed my masters working with Li-Shiuan Peh on high-performance interconnection networks. Before coming to MIT, I was an undergraduate in the Electrical Engineering department at IIT Madras.
