I am a 4th year Ph.D. candidate at CMU, co-advised by Prof. Diana Marculescu and Prof. Gauri Joshi. Before joining CMU, I received my B.Sc. degree in Electronics Engineering at Sabanci University.

I work in computer architecture, machine learning, and hardware-efficient deep learning to improve the performance of deep learning models and increase the energy-efficiency of machine learning infrastructure. My current research focuses on designing hardware-efficient deep learning models using DNN/HW co-design techniques and neural architecture search methods.
